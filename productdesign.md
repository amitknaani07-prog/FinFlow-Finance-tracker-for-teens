# FinFlow: Product Design Document

## 1. Product Vision & Overview
**FinFlow** is the money app built for teens, by a teen. Most finance apps hand control to parents, but FinFlow puts the power directly into the hands of its users. It empowers teenagers to independently manage what they earn, track their spending, and build real financial habits—without requiring a bank account or a parent dashboard watching their every move.

**Tagline:** FinFlow. Your money. Your rules.

## 2. Target Audience
- Teenagers (13-19 years old)
- Teens who earn money through non-traditional or freelance means (reselling, content creation, part-time jobs, gifts, odd jobs).
- Users who want autonomy over their financial tracking and learning.

## 3. Core Features & Functionality

### 3.1. Income & Expense Tracking
- **Youth-Centric Categories:** Custom income categories that actually make sense for teenagers (e.g., Freelance, Reselling, Content Creation, Gifts, Part-time Work, Chores).
- **Frictionless Logging:** Quick and easy input of cash and digital earnings/spendings.

### 3.2. Savings Goals
- **Visual Progress Tracking:** Users can set specific financial targets (e.g., "New Sneakers", "First Car") and see their progress fill up visually over time.
- **Milestone Celebrations:** Micro-animations when adding to a savings goal or reaching a milestone.

### 3.3. Financial Education
- **Bite-Sized Lessons:** Quick, engaging educational modules covering essential topics:
  - Budgeting 101
  - Understanding Taxes
  - Intro to Investing
- **Actionable Takeaways:** Lessons are tied directly to realistic teen financial situations to ensure relevance.

### 3.4. Gamification
- **Money Score:** An internal metric that climbs as the user practices healthy financial habits (e.g., consistent saving, completing educational modules).
- **Daily Streaks:** Encourages frequent check-ins and consistent logging of expenses and income, rewarding users with in-app achievements or points.

### 3.5. Independence & Privacy
- **No Bank Account Required:** Functions perfectly as a standalone ledger/tracker.
- **No Parent Dashboard:** Ensures complete privacy and autonomy for the teen user.
- **100% Free:** Free to start with no hidden barriers to entry.

## 5. User Journey
1. **Onboarding:** Quick, aesthetic signup without asking for banking credentials. Immediate prompt to add current cash-on-hand.
2. **Goal Setting:** User is prompted to set their first visual savings goal.
3. **Daily Use:** Logging a recent expense or new income. App updates the Money Score and maintains the daily streak.
4. **Learning:** User completes a 2-minute bite-sized lesson to boost their Money Score.
